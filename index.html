Home life

I am a Junior at Monacan Highschool. I am also the middle child out of the three kids in my household. I have an older sister and a younger brother. I would say being a middle kid has definitely influenced me in various ways. Throughout my educational career I have always had a buddy, with my sister 2 grades ahead of me and my brother 1 behind me. I have never had to face a situation in school alone. Well..until 2022. With my sister graduated and my brother at a completely different school than me, for the first time since birth I would truly face the beast of individuality. Although it has been a tedious journey of self discovery it nonetheless has been a necessary one. Despite the dependency of comradery from my siblings, independence never scared me. Being cradled between 2 people has definitely brought out the extrovert in me. When you are in the middle you tend to blend into the crowd a lot easier. So being loud and vibrant and original has constantly been my way of sticking out.
